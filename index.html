Pikachu

Pikachu is an electricity-type Pokemon, known to be powerful, mischevious - and loyal to it's friends. It's evolved form is Raichu.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a grass-type Pokemon that can commonly be found in wooded areas. It's evolved forms are Ivysuar and Venasaur.
